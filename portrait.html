Portrait Photography Tips

Great portraits depend on good lighting, angles, and subject expressions. Use soft lighting for a natural look, engage with your subject for genuine emotions, and experiment with different angles for unique shots.

Pro Tips for Stunning Portraits

  • Focus on the Eyes: The eyes convey emotion and should always be sharp.
  • Use a Wide Aperture: A lower f-stop (e.g., f/1.8) creates a beautiful background blur (bokeh).
  • Golden Hour Advantage: Shoot during sunrise or sunset for warm, flattering light.
  • Engage with Your Subject: Build rapport to capture relaxed and natural expressions.
  • Experiment with Composition: Try different angles, framing, and poses for creative results.
  • Use Reflectors or Softboxes: Enhance lighting by reducing shadows and adding depth.
