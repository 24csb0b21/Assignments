Night Photography Techniques

Capturing stunning night photos requires patience, the right camera settings, and an understanding of light behavior. With the right techniques, you can create breathtaking images filled with stars, city lights, and dreamy reflections.

Pro Tips for Amazing Night Photography

  • Use a Tripod: Stabilize your camera to prevent blurriness during long exposures.
  • Slow Shutter Speed: Increase exposure time to let in more light and capture stunning details.
  • Adjust ISO Sensibly: Start with ISO 800-1600 and increase if necessary, but beware of noise.
  • Experiment with Light Trails: Capture moving lights from cars or stars using long exposures.
  • Use Manual Focus: Autofocus struggles in low light, so manually adjust for sharp images.
  • Find Interesting Light Sources: Utilize street lamps, neon lights, or reflections for dramatic effects.
