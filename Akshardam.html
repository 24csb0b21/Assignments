History

Swaminarayan Akshardham, commonly known as Akshardham Temple, is a Hindu temple complex in Delhi, India. It was officially opened on 6 November 2005 and is dedicated to Bhagwan Swaminarayan.

The temple is known for its grand architecture and intricate carvings. The complex showcases traditional Hindu culture, spirituality, and architecture. It features exhibitions, musical fountains, and a large central monument built using pink sandstone and white marble.
